The Philosophy

What is "MosoHabit"?

Consistent habits build confidence, and confidence yields outstanding marks.

Conceptual Grounding

I do not support rote memorization. Whether we are studying specialist maths integrals or gravitational force equations in physics, I focus on understanding *why* the rules operate the way they do. Once a student grasps the fundamental concept, they can solve any curveball exam question.

Rubric-Aligned Drafting

Under the BSSS college system, school-based assessments represent 100% of your course score. I teach students how to read their assessment rubrics and draft reports that explicitly address the highest grading criteria bands (A-grade indicators).

Structured Mistake Auditing

I encourage students to keep an "Error Log" of every question they answer incorrectly in homework or practice tests. I review these logs weekly to ensure mistakes are analyzed, understood, and corrected before test day.

Timed Simulation

Being smart is only half the battle; speed and stress management are the other. As assessments approach, I simulate exam environments during weekend exercise runs to practice time allocation and test-taking composure.

Navigating Canberra's BSSS System

How ACT College scaling works and how I help students maximize their course scores.

01

Course Scores Matter

Unlike other states with central external exams, ACT scores are school-based. That means every assignment, test, and lab report counts toward your final course score. I help students maintain high consistency throughout the semesters.

02

Understanding Scaling

Your raw marks are scaled based on how your college cohort performs in the ACT Scaling Test (AST). I guide students on BSSS scaling patterns, course selections, and strategic methods to keep their positions high in the cohort rank.

03

AST Exam Readiness

The AST measures cross-curriculum skills, mathematical reasoning, and essay writing. I integrate BSSS-specific thinking skills directly into my maths and science lessons to prepare Year 12s for the scaling test.
