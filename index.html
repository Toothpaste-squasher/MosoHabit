The Philosophy

What is "MosoHabit"?

Consistent habits build confidence, and confidence yields outstanding marks.

Depth over Pace

No matter how fast you race through topics, your knowledge can easily collapse like a tall building without a solid foundation, causing more harm than good. I prioritise the depth and quality of your understanding over speed. We take the time to patiently build a strong foundation on the whys—why concepts, formulas, and principles work—before applying them to questions. It might take more time and be brain-draining at first, but when paired with practice, it properly sets you up for the explosive academic growth in the future.

Long-Lasting Habits

Outstanding marks are built on consistent habits, but you can only build a long-lasting habit if you actually enjoy the journey. Discipline can be helpful, but let’s be honest, it might only last a few hours or days—it isn’t always there. I have been there too; I really get it. When I started truly enjoying Maths and Science, studying didn’t feel like a hassle anymore, but a natural, sustainable part of my every day entertainment. I will try my best to guide you, so that the process of getting good grades fun and rewarding every step of the way.

Embrace Failure

Mistakes are what I aim for when practising. Exercise sessions are the safest place to mess up so we can tackle the errors together, before they appear in the exams. Treat failures not as setbacks, but as learning opportunities to improve and grow your future self!

BSSS Mastery

Under the BSSS college system, school-based assessments make up 100% of your course score. Success also comes by closely following the rules of the game. I will teach you how to make excellent use of your drafts and properly dissect assessment rubrics, ensuring your hard work perfectly hits the exact criteria the marking panels are looking for.
